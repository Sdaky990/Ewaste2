Proper Disposal Methods

Disposing of electronic waste responsibly prevents environmental damage and recovers valuable resources. The best ways to dispose of e-waste include:

  • Using certified e-waste recycling centers
  • Participating in community collection events
  • Donating working electronics to charities or schools
  • Returning electronics to manufacturers or retailers for recycling

Health and Safety Protocols

E-waste contains hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium. Safe handling is essential to protect workers and the community:

  • Always wear gloves, masks, and protective eyewear when handling broken electronics
  • Never dismantle devices at home—this should be done at certified facilities
  • Keep e-waste away from children and food areas
  • Ensure recyclers follow environmental and health standards
